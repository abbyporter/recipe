Chocolate Chip Muffins

Background

Chocolate Chip Muffins are quick and easy to make! When I was little I loved to make chocolate chip muffins with my grandma. They were always the best in the morning with milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

    • Steps

  • Heat oven to 400 degrees F and grease bottoms only of baking cups
  • In a medium bowl combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, chocolate chips, and salt
  • Combine milk, oil, and egg and mix
  • Add dry ingredients to previous step and stir until moist
  • Fill baking liners 2/3 full, sprinkle tops of muffins before baking with 3 tablespoons sugar and 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, let cool for 1 minute before serving.
    • Allergy Information:
  • Wheat
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Soybean
  • Cocoa
